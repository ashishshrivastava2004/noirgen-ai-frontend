## 🚀 About Neon Noir Labs & Our Vision
At Neon Noir Labs, we understand the heavy lifting required in modern creative workflows. Translating raw script ideas into production-ready visual treatments—whether for short films, high-end editorial shoots, or digital ad campaigns—is traditionally expensive and time-consuming. 

**NoirGen AI** was built to solve this bottleneck. We bridge the gap between raw creative ideation and technical execution, allowing directors and designers to generate highly structured, studio-grade cinematic lookbooks in seconds.

## ✨ Core Innovations
* 🧠 **Cinematic JSON Engine:** Powered by Gemini 1.5, our backend extracts deep narrative themes and translates them into technical cinematography data (precise lighting ratios, focal lengths, and color palettes).
* 📄 **1-Click PDF Lookbook Export:** NoirGen doesn't just generate images; it instantly compiles the AI-generated visual treatment into a professional, shareable PDF document ready to be handed to a DP or VFX crew.
* 🎨 **Curated Aesthetic Control:** Built to target specific, high-end directorial styles (like the deep, melancholic lighting of a Wong Kar-wai film). 
* 🎯 **Strict Visual Standards:** Our underlying prompt architecture enforces a clean, modern editorial aesthetic. For example, it intelligently styles subjects with contemporary accessories like modern headphones, while ensuring clean, artifact-free faces (expressly filtering out specific markings or red tilaks) to maintain a consistent, global cinematic standard.

## 🛠️ Architecture & Tech Stack
* **Frontend UI/UX:** React, Vite, Tailwind CSS
* **Reasoning Engine:** Gemini AI via Google AI Studio
* **Hosting & Serverless:** Vercel
